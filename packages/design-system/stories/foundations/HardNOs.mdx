# Hard NOs

These are the AI-default design tells we are explicitly killing.
Any pull request that introduces any of the following is rejected.

---

### 1. No AI purple, violet, or deep indigo

No `#0F0F2D`, no `#6366F1`, no synthwave palette anywhere.

### 2. No purple-to-pink or blue-to-purple gradients

Gradients are reserved for `gradient.page.foot` — one vertical, single-hue, low-contrast fade.
No diagonal gradients. No multi-hue gradients.

### 3. No electric blue or hot pink accents

No `#3B82F6` ("Lovable blue"). No `#E61363` ("Lovable/Vercel pink"). No "default SaaS" accents.

### 4. No generic AI off-whites as canvas

Not `#FCFBF8`, not `#F4F3EE`, not `#F7F4ED`. Canvas is `#EBE7DC`. Use it exactly.

### 5. No Inter, no Geist

Typography is Söhne/General Sans + Berkeley Mono/Commit Mono. No Google Fonts CDN.
Self-host only.

### 6. No glassmorphism

Surfaces are solid. Borders do the work. The **one exception**: the sticky `<TopNav>` on scroll
uses `backdrop-filter` with `opacity ≥ 0.85`. That is the only place in the entire system.

### 7. No pure black or violet-black

`color.ink.primary` is `#1F1B16` — warm brown-black. Dark mode (v2+) will use a warm-neutral
palette, never violet-tinted.

### 8. No cookie-cutter SaaS hero layout

No: centered headline + three-column feature grid + giant pricing table.
Use `<Specsheet>`, `<Manifesto>`, `<WorkLog>`, and asymmetric `<Grid>` compositions.

### 9. No ink/terracotta/amber/charcoal as dominant palette

Those are the "operator-warm" clichés. This system uses Workshop (§4 of the brief).

### 10. No emoji as decorative bullets

Emoji is allowed in copy. Never as iconography, never as `<Eyebrow>` decoration.
Use Lucide icons via `<Icon>` at 1.5px stroke only.
