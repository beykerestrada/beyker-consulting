## Signal

The **single accent** in this system. Workshop green appears on the logo, primary buttons,
active nav items, link underlines, and pullquote rules. If more than ~6 green elements
are visible simultaneously on a page, something is wrong.

## Gradient policy

One gradient is permitted: `gradient.page.foot` — a vertical fade from canvas to sunken,
used only at the bottom of full-page sections to suggest depth.

```css
background: var(--bk-gradient-page-foot);
/* = linear-gradient(to bottom, #EBE7DC, #DDD8C9) */
```

No diagonal gradients. No multi-hue gradients.
